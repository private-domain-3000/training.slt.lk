Electronics & Communication

Provides high quality knowledge and skills in a broad range of topics necessary for professional Electronics and Communication Engineers. This program will basically provide knowledge on principles of Electronics and Communication systems,

Intellectual skills to design Electronics and Communication systems and practical skills to perform experimental work in Electronics and Communication Engineering

Entry Requirements

02 simple passes in Mathematics or Technological streams in local or London A-Levels.

Credit pass for the English language in Local or London O-levels or Simple pass in Local or London A-Levels

Other qualifications including BTEC National Diploma or any other equivalent qualification
