Established in 1973, the Moratuwa Training centre is the first training centre of SLT. The training Centre is located, in Horethuduwa along Old Galle Road and is easily accessible from Kalutara, Piliyandala and Colombo.

Moratuwa Training Centre has all required facilities including Laboratories , Classrooms, Auditorium, Library and Administration office. It also has a hostel and Playground facilities for Cricket, Volley Ball, Badminton and indoor games such as Table Tennis, Carom and Chessâ€™. Gymnasium also available for students use.

Computer use & Internet access
  • Moratuwa Training Centre consists of three fully air conditioned computer labs for general computer trainings - two with 25 work stations and the other with 10 work stations.
  • All three labs are fully networked and have high speed internet connectivity via SLT Broadband.
Laboratory facilities
  • Optical Fiber lab
    • Moratuwa Training Centre consists of a fully air conditioned lab facility for Optical Fiber cabling trainings. This Optical Fiber lab is equipped with Splicing machines, Optical Time-Domain Reflectometer, Power meter, different types of OF cables and OF Closures.
  • Outside plant training bay
    • Moratuwa Training Centre consists of an Outside plant training bay and lab facility for Outside plant Copper cabling trainings. This Outside plant training bay and lab facility is equipped with equipments, tools and materials used for laying, jointing and enclosing of both aerial and underground copper cables.
    • These lab facilities are furnished in the optimum way possible to reflect a pleasant as well as dynamic and modern environment, whilst ensuring comfort and complying with health standards.
Auditorium
  • The Auditorium at Moratuwa Training Centre with a capacity of 70 seats is fully air conditioned and equipped with a surround sound system with clip-on mic, multimedia projector with a screen, a podium, flip chart board and white board.
Library
  • Library at Moratuwa Training Centre provides access to more than 2000 books on different disciplines such as Telecommunication, Data communication, Mobile communication, Optical Fiber communication, Computer Networks, Electronics, Mathematics, etc.

Inaugurated in 1988, the Welisara Training Centre is one of our main training centres. Situated in the Welisara, Negombo Road, it is easily accessible from Colombo, Negombo as well as Gampaha.

The Welisara Training Centre premises comprise of all required facilities specially designed to ensure a pleasant learning environment. It consists of fully air conditioned labs, classrooms, auditorium, library and administration offices. A hostel facility is also available for students, as well as a large sports ground and an indoor games room to encourage students to take part in sports activities and thus ensuring a vibrant campus life.

Computer use & Internet access
  • Four fully equipped computer labs with Internet connectivity complying with modern environment and standards.
Laboratory facilities
  • Data communication with Multi Protocols Label Switching
    • Equipped with all required instruments and facilities to simulate a practical environment for Routing, Switching and VOIP trainings ( CISCO,L2 Switching).
    • Equipped with all required instruments and facilities to simulate a practical environment for the trainings related to Leased Line Internet, MPLS,CCTV and Advanced Routing (CISCO).
  • Sun Certified Training
    • Training facilities for Solaris.
  • Instrumentation
    • Facilities available for practical assignments in telecommunication networks with all standard Instruments required for maintenance.
  • Transmission Networks
    • Facilities available for practical assignments in transmission network technologies such as SDH,PDH and other IP based technologies.
  • PSTN and Switching lab
    • Facilities available for practical assignments in PSTN telephony and Switching.
Library

The library consists of books and material on a wide range of subjects including Telecommunications, Electrical Engineering, Electronics, Computer Science, Civil Engineering, Mathematics, Management, and English Language.

Library Facilities include reference, lending, periodicals and newspapers and audio visual sections. It also maintains a store of of books, reports, circulars, manuals, gray literature, periodicals, data books and standards as well as audio visual materials (VHS, VCD, DVD, audio tapes).

Libraries are open on week days 9.00 am to 4.30 pm.
