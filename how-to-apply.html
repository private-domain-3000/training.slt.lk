How To Apply ?

Thank you for your interest in applying to the Sri Lanka Telecom Training Center. We have simplified the application process to ensure a smooth and efficient experience for all applicants. Follow the steps below to submit your application:

  1. Step 1: Review the Course Catalog: Explore our comprehensive course catalog to identify the training program that aligns with your career goals and interests. Take note of the program's prerequisites, duration, and any specific requirements.

  2. Step 2: Prepare Required Documents: Gather the necessary documents required for the application process. This may include your educational certificates, identification documents, and any other supporting materials specified in the course application guidelines.

  3. Step 3: Complete the Application Form: Visit our online application portal or download the application form from our website. Fill in the required information accurately and ensure that all supporting documents are attached as per the instructions provided.

  4. Step 4: Submit Your Application: Once you have completed the application form and attached all the required documents, submit your application through the designated channel. This may involve uploading the documents online or mailing them to the specified address.

  5. Step 5: Application Review and Confirmation: Our admissions team will review your application and assess your eligibility for the chosen program. If further information or an interview is required, our team will contact you to schedule the necessary steps. Once your application is approved, you will receive an official confirmation of enrollment.

  6. Step 6: Payment of Fees: After receiving the confirmation of enrollment, you will be provided with detailed information regarding the program fees and payment methods. Ensure that you complete the payment process within the specified timeframe to secure your spot in the program.

  7. Step 7: Orientation and Program Start: Prior to the program start date, you will be invited to attend an orientation session to familiarize yourself with the training center, facilities, and program expectations. The program will commence as scheduled, and you will embark on an exciting learning journey with us.

We look forward to receiving your application and welcoming you to the Sri Lanka Telecom Training Center. Should you have any questions or require further assistance during the application process, please don't hesitate to contact our admissions team.
