Peradeniya Study Center

The Peradeniya Training Centre which was established in 1983,located along the Kandy - Colombo Road at the Galaha Junction (Old Galaha Road) near the Peradeniya Botanical Gardens. The Peradeniya Training Centre has all required facilities including modern state of the art Laboratories , Classrooms, Auditorium, Library and Administration office.
