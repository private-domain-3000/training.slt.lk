BTEC

The BTEC HND is a pre-degree, professional qualification awarded by Pearson, UK. It is a globally recognized prestigious vocational qualification, preparing students for both Degree education and Employment. Qualifiers can get admissions to the 3rd year of B.Sc. or B.Eng. in more than 100 universities in UK, USA or Australia.

HND in Electrical & Electronic Engineering

Entry level qualification most suitable for those who wish to begin their career in the fields of Electrical Engineering and Electronic Engineering, such as school leavers and who are looking to obtain professional qualifications. Students will be taught the fundamentals of Electrical Engineering and Electronic technologies.

Course Contents

Minimum Entry Requirements:

  • GCE (A/L) or London (A/L) in Mathematics,Bio-science or Technology streams.
  • Completion of NVQ level 4
  • Completion of Engineering Foundation Course.
  • Other qualifications will be considered by the course team in consultation with the SLT Training Centre Management.

Application Procedure:

You may fill the official application form and submit to the Training Centre, along with the following proof documents:

  • Copy of National Identity Card
  • Copy of Birth Certificate
  • Certified copies of your previous education

HND in Digital Technologies

Pearson BTEC Higher Nationals in Digital Technologies is for those who wish to develop as professional, self-reflecting individuals able to meet the demands of employers in the computing sector and adapt to a constantly changing world. The qualification aim to widen access to higher education and enhance the career prospects of those who undertake them.

Course Contents

Specialist Pathways :

  • Network Engineering
  • Software Engineering
  • Data Analytics
  • Security
  • Intelligent Systems
  • Applications Development

Minimum Entry Requirements:

  • GCE A/L or London A/L with 2 passes in any stream or equivalent & Credit pass for English at GCE O/L
    or
  • Successfully complete a Certificate course in Information Technology Programme (CAIT) at Telecom Training Centre & Credit pass for English at GCE O/L.
